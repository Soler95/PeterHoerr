About
Peter Hörr

Cellist and Conductor Peter Hoerr is regarded as one of the most versatile and sought-after German artists of his generation. An early interest in ancient music led him to seminal studies with Heinrich Schiff and Christophe Coin at the Basel Music Academy.

Prize winner at the 1989 Scheveningen International Cello Competition, he has performed at major venues including Concertgebouw Amsterdam, Lucerne Festival, Wigmore Hall and Berlin Philharmonie.

He was appointed Artistic Director and Principal Conductor of the Hofkapelle Weimar in 2009. The ensemble records exclusively for MDG.
