Biography

About
Peter Hörr

Peter Hörr is one of Germany's most respected cellists, known for his refined sound, artistic depth, and an international concert career spanning three decades and four continents.

Since 2000, he is Professor of Cello at the renowned Felix Mendelssohn Bartholdy University of Music and Theatre Leipzig, one of Germany's leading institutions for higher musical education.
