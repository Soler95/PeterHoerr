Biography

A distinguished voice of the European cello tradition

Peter Hörr is one of Germany's leading cellists, renowned for his refined artistry, chamber music collaborations and extensive international concert activity.

Having studied with Heinrich Schiff and Christophe Coin, he has performed in major international venues including the Lincoln Center in New York, Wigmore Hall in London and Suntory Hall in Tokyo.

Alongside an acclaimed recording career, he serves as Professor of Violoncello and Chamber Music at the Hochschule für Musik und Theater Felix Mendelssohn Bartholdy Leipzig.
