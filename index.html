Biography

About
Peter Hörr

Peter Hörr is one of Germany's most respected cellists, known for his refined sound, artistic depth, and an international concert career spanning three decades and four continents.

A graduate of the Hochschule für Musik Detmold, Peter studied under legendary masters before establishing himself as a soloist of rare sensitivity — equally at home in Baroque repertoire and contemporary commissions.
